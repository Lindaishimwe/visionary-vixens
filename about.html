About Us

Welcome to Tales & Tea – Your Gateway to Knowledge, Community, and Inspiration.

At Tales & Tea, we believe in the transformative power of books, learning, and shared experiences.

Our Mission

To empower our community through:

  • Free access to books, digital resources, and cutting-edge technology.
  • Lifelong learning programs for all ages (storytimes, workshops, tech classes).
  • Inclusivity—a welcoming space where everyone belongs.
